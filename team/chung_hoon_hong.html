About

Chung Hoon Hong is a graduate student pursuing a Master's in Data Science. Chung Hoon has NLP experience with image caption generation and text-based sentiment classification, and will focus on natural language generation. One past project was the decoding of myoelectric signals of the arm to predict finger movements successfully using neural ordinary differential equations.
