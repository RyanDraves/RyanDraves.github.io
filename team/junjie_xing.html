About

Junjie Xing is a Master's student in Computer Science and Engineering with NLP experience in Chinese word segmentation, named entity recognition, and relation extraction. Past projects include a project on medical knowledge base construction, and Junjie will focus on the knowledge base aspects of the socialbot.


Publication: Xing, J., Zhu, K., & Zhang, S. (2018, August). Adaptive Multi-Task Transfer Learning for Chinese Word Segmentation in Medical Text. In Proceedings of the 27th International Conference on Computational Linguistics (pp. 3619-3630).
