About

Yiting Shen is a Master's student in Computer Science & Engineering. Yiting has previous NLP experience with deep-learning language identification and a part-of-speech tagging project with the Viterbi algorithm. Other past projects include measuring personal values via user-generated content. Yiting will focus on sentiment analysis and computational social science.


Publication: Wilson, S. R., Shen, Y., & Mihalcea, R. (2018, September). Building and Validating Hierarchical Lexicons with a Case Study on Personal Values. In International Conference on Social Informatics (pp. 455-470). Springer, Cham. (Best Paper Award)
