Our Vision

That first conversation when you meet a best friend or even fall in love: We trot out our best stories to make a great first impression and ask questions connect with the other person while avoiding uncomfortable silences. We share more personal details about ourselves, and the conversation grows in depth. These conversations make us feel valued as people by allowing us to be ourselves and move beyond pleasantries to talk about things we might not normally. However, current conversations with socialbots fall short, driven by short scripts that mindlessly regurgitate facts about movies, technology, and sports. Our vision is to build a socialbot that connects with users through active listening and, through mutual storytelling, builds a meaningful connection based who they are as a person.
